"A dreamy view just like the one we all have when we sleep"

YASMIN ZAINUDIN LANGKAWI FRI, DEC 2021 7

Travelling to places gives us a chance in experiencing new wonders. Beautiful views and scenarios that can only be experienced with our only eyes speak more than a thousand words. Souls that search for peace can be found in moments we see something so beautioful, so admiring.

A picture taken at this hour and location displays a silhouette of people enjoying their foods with their companionship. Truly a dreamy view for those who appreciate sunset.

Everything feels like it falls into its place the longer you look at this pciture because at the end of the day, deep down in our hearts, we all know we truthfully belong somewhere in this beatufiul world.
